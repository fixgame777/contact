The logos are embedded as inline SVG, so they will always look sharp. You can easily change colors, sizes, or add backgrounds.

To use your own images instead:
Replace the entire <svg>...</svg> with an <img> tag, e.g.:
<img src="your-whatsapp-logo.png" width="120" height="120" alt="WhatsApp">

Make sure your images are high-resolution (preferably PNG with transparency) for the best result.

⚙️ Adjust spacing, colors, and background in the <style> section to match your brand.
